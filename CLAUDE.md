# King's Group Academies — 1:1 iPad Scheme Website

Interactive single-page HTML website promoting the King's Group Academies 1:1 iPad scheme for parents.

## About the Project

A standalone HTML file (no frameworks, no dependencies) that acts as a parent-facing information and sign-up page for the school's iPad leasing programme.

## Brand & Style

- **Colours:** Navy and gold throughout
- **School motto:** "Opportunity and success on a global stage" — appears in the hero banner and footer
- **Tone:** Professional, reassuring, parent-friendly
- **Layout:** Responsive — must work on mobile, tablet, and desktop

## Site Sections

1. **Hero** — School name, motto, and "Sign up" CTA button
2. **Our Vision** — Educational rationale for the 1:1 iPad model
3. **Device & Accessories** — Full product breakdown (see below)
4. **Pricing** — Four payment options (24-month highlighted as most popular)
5. **Q&A** — Expandable accordion, 12 questions
6. **Contact** — Placeholder fields for school contact details and sign-up portal link

## Device & Programme Details

**Device & Accessories:**
- Apple iPad 11-inch 128GB Wi-Fi
- USB-C 20W charger
- Deqster Smart Rugged Touch Plus keyboard case
- Tucano Pencil 3

**Programme Cover (all 3-year, no excess):**
- Mechanical warranty
- Accidental damage cover
- Edutech parent programme support
- Direct Debit management
- Jamf licence
- Operating lease structure with end-of-term transfer of ownership to the family

**Additional Option:**
- Screen protector fitted before delivery: £8 per device
